Wisconsin Coastal Bluff Erosion

Scroll to Continue

Bluff Erosion is a significant issue affecting coastal communities in Wisconsin. Although scientists have studied the Great Lakes closely, this work often fails to reach the broader public. Coastal bluff erosion is complex and occurs over a long time scale. As such, many people who are affected by this natural hazard do not fully appreciate either the inherent instability of desirable lakefront property or how some land-use practices make the coast less stable. This website aims to stimulate a deeper understanding of the dynamic and unstable nature of Wisconsin's coast.
