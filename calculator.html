What is a stable slope?

The stability of a slope is based on bluff height and material.

Bluff height can be categorized as high (over 20m) or low (under 20m).

Bluffs can be comprised of sandy soils, cohesive soils, or bedrock. Bluffs can be simple, and comprise only a single material, or complex, comprising multiple materials.

Different materials in different places have different stable angles.

  • Lake Michigan — Cohesive Soil
  • Lake Superior — Cohesive Soil
  • Sandy Soil
